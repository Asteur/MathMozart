Transfer Files
	From EC2 to PC
		scp -i ~/Desktop/amazon.pem ubuntu@ec2-52-25-130-31.us-west-2.compute.amazonaws.com:/data/ecoli_ref-5m-trim.fastq.gz ~/Download/
	From PC to EC2
		scp -i /home/ubuntu/Downloads/firsttest.pem ~/Desktop/MS115.fa  ubuntu@ec2-52-25-130-31.us-west-2.compute.amazonaws.com:/home/ubuntu

Open Instance
	ssh -i "firsttest.pem" ubuntu@ec2-52-25-130-31.us-west-2.compute.amazonaws.com

Open jupyter in chrome
	https://ubuntu@ec2-52-25-130-31.us-west-2.compute.amazonaws.com:8888

Convert pem to ppk
	sudo apt install putty
	chmod a+x firsttest.pem
	puttygen firsttest.pem -O private -o firsttest.ppk
	chmod 400 firsttest.pem
	chmod 400 firsttest.ppk
